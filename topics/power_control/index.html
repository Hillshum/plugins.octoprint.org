Power Control

Topics are curated lists of plugins pertaining to a certain topic. They are provided for your convenience, they do not constitute official endorsement of any plugin.

The following plugins allow controlling power to your printer via OctoPrint through various means.

  • PSU Control

    25 Jan 2017 AGPLv3 7.61k 182 17 Aug 2021 1.0.6 (12 May 2021)

    Smart control of your power supply via GPIO, GCODE Command, System Command, or variety of sub-plugins.

    atx control gpio power psu psucontrol

  • OctoPrint-TPLinkSmartplug

    03 Sep 2017 AGPLv3 4.07k 104 18 Mar 2023 1.0.3 (18 Mar 2023)

    Plugin to control TP-Link Smartplug devices from OctoPrint web interface.

    power smartplug tp-link tplink

  • OctoPrint-Tasmota

    24 Nov 2017 AGPLv3 1.7k 58 06 Mar 2023 1.1.3 (15 Oct 2022)

    Simple plugin to control sonoff devices that have been flashed with Tasmota.

    power smartplug sonoff tasmota

  • OctoRelay

    13 Mar 2021 AGPLv3 1.39k 36 11 Nov 2021 1.2.0 (11 Nov 2021)

    A plugin to control relays or other things on the GPIO pins of your raspberry pi. For example turn the power of printer, the light or a fan ON and OFF via the web interface.

    gpio power raspberry pi relay switch

  • ShutdownPrinter

    18 Jun 2018 AGPLv3 977 16 21 May 2022 1.0.8 (22 Nov 2019)

    Plugin for shutdown printer after finishing a print job

    shutdown shutdown printer tplink

  • OctoPrint-TpLinkAutoShutdown

    17 Feb 2021 AGPLv3 681 8 30 Jul 2022 1.3.0 (19 Sep 2021)

    This plugin is designed to help you integrate your IoT TP-Link Kasa wireless plug into OctoPrint. The basis of the plug-in is to enable you to automatically switch-off your 3D printer once a print has successfully completed.

    auto_shutdown kasa printer tp-link

  • OctoPrint-TasmotaMQTT

    17 Jan 2018 AGPLv3 526 17 06 Mar 2023 0.3.9 (22 Oct 2021)

    Plugin to control Tasmota devices via MQTT protocol.

    mqtt power tasmota

  • OctoPrint-TuyaSmartplug

    12 Nov 2018 AGPLv3 495 17 13 Oct 2020 0.3.0 (16 Oct 2020)

    Plugin to control Tuya based Smartplug devices from OctoPrint web interface.

    power smartplug tuya

  • Ophom

    16 Aug 2020 AGPLv3 403 7 28 Sep 2022 1.5.1 (28 Sep 2022)

    Octoprint Philips Hue Outlet Manager

    auto off hue outlet philips power manage

  • OctoPrint-WemoSwitch

    18 Sep 2018 AGPLv3 302 11 06 Mar 2023 0.1.11 (15 Oct 2022)

    Plugin to control Belkin Wemo devices from OctoPrint web interface.

    belkin power smartplug wemo

  • OctoPrint-MyStromSwitch

    03 Dec 2019 AGPLv3 53 4 25 Jul 2020 1.2.0 (25 Jul 2020)

    Plugin to integrate myStrom Switch into your OctoPrint installation

    mystrom relay switch

  • OctoPrint-EnergenieControl

    05 Apr 2021 AGPLv3 32 0 19 Feb 2022 v1.0.2 (19 Feb 2022)

    Controls Energenie sockets to turn printer on and off.

    gpio power raspberry pi wireless

  • OctoPrint-OrviboS20

    03 Oct 2019 AGPLv3 12 2 14 Jun 2020 0.2.3 (14 Jun 2020)

    Plugin to control Orvibo S20 Smart Plug from the OctoPrint web interface.

    orvibo power s20 smartplug

  • ATXPiHat

    25 Jan 2018 Creative Commons 0 6 08 Jul 2018 1.1.5 (17 Jun 2018)

    Software support for the ATXPiHat

    atx control emergency epo fan led monitor psu
